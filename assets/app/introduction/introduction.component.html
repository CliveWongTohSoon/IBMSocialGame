INTRODUCTION

SpaceRaze is a Star Trek inspired game that uses a player's twitter personality to adjust for different experiences. The game uses IBM Watson AI personality insight module to analyse the player's personality through their twitter account. It returns a score for each personality attribute in the Five Factor Model and the Theory of Basic Human Values. Then the game uses an algorithm to convert these scores into 5 different ship parameters. Each ship within a game represents one player.


HOW TO PLAY

  • Your aim is to find and destroy the other spaceship, before it happens to you!

  • The radar system of your ship will detect nearby enemy ships and asteroids by emitting a “ping” noise in the direction which they are coming from, increasing pitch the closer it gets.

  • Your ship has the ability to do the following things:

    • SHOOT
    • MOVE
    • TURN - Left/Right
    • SHIELD - Front/Back/Left/Right

  • You have 60 seconds to give a maximum of 3 commands to your crew. Along with the original commands, the ship can also accept some variations of these commands:

    • “FIRE” or “BLAST” for “SHOOT”
    • “ROTATE” or “SPIN” for “TURN”
    • “DEFEND”, “ACTIVATE FORCE FIELD”, “ARMOUR UP”, or “COVER” for “SHIELD”
    • “FORWARD” or “STRAIGHT AHEAD” for "MOVE"

      • There are several others- feel free to try them!

  • At the end of the 60 seconds, the commands of both players will be executed.
